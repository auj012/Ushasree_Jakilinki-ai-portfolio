## How I work

- **Problem first, AI second.** I validate the problem before deciding whether AI is the right solution.
- **Data honesty.** I verify the required data exists before designing a model.
- **Evaluation is part of the product.** I design evaluation strategies alongside the solution, focusing on guardrails, groundedness, precision, recall, and explainability.
- **Build to learn.** I prototype ideas to validate assumptions, uncover technical risks, and iterate on product decisions.
